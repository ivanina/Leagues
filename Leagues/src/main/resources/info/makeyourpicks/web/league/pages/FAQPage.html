Frequently asked questions
  • Q: What if the league creator fails to make the the payment before the start of the season?
  • A: If the payment is not received before the first game of season an email notification will be sent to the league creator. Players will still be able to play the first week of the season. If the payment is not received before the end of the first week the league will be cancelled.
